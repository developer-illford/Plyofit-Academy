WELCOME TO
PLYO FIT - Fitness Academy

where your journey to become a top-tier fitness professional begins.

We provide comprehensive education and training for aspiring fitness trainers, combining theoretical knowledge and practical skills. Our mission is to develop highly qualified and passionate fitness professionals who can deliver scientifically sound training to improve physical fitness of their clients.

PLYO FIT - Trainers can earn nationally and globally recognized certificate programs with the assistance of Fitness Academy.

Our curriculum ensures a well-rounded education, focusing on developing the expertise needed to excel in the fitness industry with a holistic approach.

QUALITY TRAINING BY QUALIFIED PROFESSIONALS

Meet Our Founder: Mr. Vipin O V

Mr. Vipin O V, a veteran soldier from the Indian Armed Forces, founded PLYO FIT - Fitness Academy in 2017. Vipin served in the Indian Air Force for 15 years as a sportsman and fitness trainer. His fitness journey began in school, transforming his physique to overcome teasing and prove his capabilities. At seventeen, he joined the military to pursue his passion for fitness and adventure.

Throughout his twenties, Vipin earned medals in Air Force, Services and Senior National bodybuilding championships. He continued his education in health, fitness, and physical education, qualifying as a tutor and assessor for international fitness trainer certifications and passing the Kerala government Teacher Eligibility Test (KTET).

Vipin’s dream is to enhance societal health & fitness by creating energetic and qualified professional trainers. PLYO FIT, named from "PLYO" (increase in Latin) and "FIT" (fitness), embodies his vision of elevating fitness standards.

Join PLYO FIT - Fitness Academy today and transform your passion for fitness into a professional reality under the guidance of our expert team and founder, Mr. Vipin O V.
